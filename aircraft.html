How to read it

The radar updates continuously from public ADS-B transponder data broadcast by aircraft worldwide.

01

Color

Aircraft are typically colored by altitude band — lower colors for aircraft on approach, brighter for cruise altitude.

02

Trails

The thin line behind each aircraft icon is its recent ground track — useful for spotting holding patterns near busy airports.

03

Click to inspect

Click any aircraft icon directly on the radar for its flight number, origin, destination, speed, and altitude.
